About us

High quality coffee

We love our coffee. We believe that genuine emotions can be conveyed and shared through a high-quality cup of artisanal coffee, and such a simple act may provide some positive energy for customers. Therefore, "Coffee & Tea" has always been committed to ethically sourcing, roasting and serving high-quality Arabica coffee beans. We have also established the "Coffee and Tea" China Coffee Growers Support Center in Pu'er, Yunnan, China, to integrate and promote sustainable farming practices in the local area and support the development of the coffee industry in Yunnan. From a seed to a cup of coffee, every link is poured into the heart and effort of "coffee and tea", so that every customer who visits our store can enjoy the mellow espresso and its favorite coffee drink.

A one-of-a-kind experience

We love our customers. Coffee & Tea is committed to providing customers with a consistently high-quality experience. At the same time, on the basis of product quality and service excellence, "coffee and tea" emphasizes innovation, emphasizes the personalization of products and services, and emphasizes constantly bringing pleasure and surprise to customers. We also fully respect China's time-honored traditional culture, and perfectly integrate local customs into the "coffee and tea" experience in terms of store design and local food and beverage serving.
