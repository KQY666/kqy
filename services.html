Our Services

How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

Personal information refers to all kinds of information recorded electronically or otherwise that can identify a specific natural person alone or in combination with other information or reflect the activities of a specific natural person. Personal sensitive information refers to personal information that, once leaked, illegally provided or abused, may endanger personal and property safety, and can easily lead to damage to personal reputation, physical and mental health, or discriminatory treatment. If the information we collect and use involves your sensitive personal information, we will highlight it with bold font and underline to clearly identify you. At the same time, we will take encryption and other security measures to transmit and store your sensitive personal information.

Help you complete the account registration

When you register as a member of our platform or log in to our platform client, you need to provide us with at least your mobile phone number and the verification code you received, and we will help you complete the verification process of login and registration through SMS. If you only need to use basic services such as browsing and searching, you do not need to register as a member and provide the above information.

Help you browse and search for products

When you browse product/service information and search for nearby stores, we need to collect your device information to display the product/service information of nearby stores and stores in the best way for you.When you use the product/service information search, "favorite taste", "taste customization", shopping cart function, we need to collect your browsing history, collection record, customization record, additional purchase information, order information.

Help you complete order placement and order management

When you add items to your shopping cart and check out the items in your shopping cart, we will generate an order for you to purchase the relevant items. At a minimum, you need to provide us with your consignee's name, mobile phone number, delivery address, and the order will state the order type, order number, order time, information about the goods you purchased, the amount you should pay, the method of your payment, and the time of order, pick-up time and other relevant information, all of which constitute your "order information".
