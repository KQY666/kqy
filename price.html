Passion and creativity."Coffee & Tea" launches the Ice Shock Concentrate Series for the first time

What is a whole new level of deep roasted Arabica? Imagine when piping hot fresh espresso is poured on ice cubes - the characteristic aroma of dark roasted espresso is instantly pervasive and rapidly condensed; Afterwards, the barista shakes the ice cubes and coffee with enthusiasm, and with the symphony of ice and fire, the coffee will glow with light foam; The final injection of oat milk brings a blend of marbling and varied texture layers...

Today, our store announced the launch of a new iced coffee category - "Ice Shock Concentrate" series. Two coffee drinks, Sea Salt Caramel Ice Shock Concentrate and Tahitian Vanilla Style Ice Shock Concentrate, made using the next-generation "Ice Shake" coffee blending technology, landed in all Chinese mainland stores. The "Ice Shock Concentrate" series will also become our new core coffee category, bringing customers a different "ice shock" charm of espresso throughout the year.
